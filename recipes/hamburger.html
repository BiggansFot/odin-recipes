Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a perfect meal for the hungry person. It is (relatively) fast to make, flexible with ingredients and tasty for even the most picky eater.

Excluding the majority of ingredients, here is a list of what you need

Ingredients 4 burgers

  • Bread (burger buns are not a necessity)
  • 800g minced meat (preferably ground chuck)
  • Cheese slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Whatever other topping of your choice

Steps

  1. Begin by dividing the meat into four equal balls. Put some oil in a pan on medium heat and place the balls of meat in it. Immediately press them flat with a spatula and fry them on both sides until the middle is no longer red.
  2. When the patties is done grab the sliced cheese and place them on top of the patties and leave them on the pan until the cheese has melted.
  3. Place a slice of bread on four plates. Spread out some mayonnaise and place the finished patties on top.
  4. Place whatever other toppings you'd like (salad leaves, pickles, onion and bbq-sauce for example).
  5. Top the burger with a second slice of bread and serve with fries.
